About this Pen

ℹ️ Scroll-Triggered Animations are animations that are triggered when scrolling past a certain position. Once triggered, these animations start and finish on their own, independent of any future scroll position.

👉 A typical example is content that flies/fades in as you scroll across the page. Once it's in view the animation starts and there's no stopping it.

☑️ In this demo you can't scroll yourself, but can fake a scroll using the “Set Scroll Position to Bottom” control at the top left.
